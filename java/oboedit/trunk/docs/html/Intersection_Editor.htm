The Intersection Editor

The purpose of this panel is to provide an editable tabular view of all of the cross product terms in the ontology, listed alphabetically. For each term is lists the ID, name, genus, differentia, and a logical expression of the relationships. The Intersection Editor is accessed from the Editors menu.

The term in focus can be linked to the other panels in OBO-Edit by selecting the 'link this selection with main selection' button if required. Otherwise the cross-product terms in the table can be browsed independently of what is being displayed in the other panels. Upon selection of a term by double clicking on it, an editing panel will appear within the table. To exit this panel, click on the table.

Terms in this panel are edited in the same way as in the cross-product tab of the Text Editor. There are two additional options:

  • The "anonymous" checkbox
  • The "Autogenerate name" button
